## 2. Typography

### Primary Pairing: Cormorant + Josefin Sans *(Sweet & Elegant)*

- **Headings:** *Cormorant* (Medium/SemiBold) — Delicate, high-fashion serif
- **Body:** *Josefin Sans* (Light/Regular) — Geometric with vintage charm
- **Vibe:** Sweet, feminine, modern artisan

### Alternate Pairing: Cormorant Garamond + Montserrat *(More Editorial)*

- **Headings:** *Cormorant Garamond* (Medium/SemiBold) — Fashion-editorial elegance
- **Body:** *Montserrat* (Light/Regular) — Clean, highly legible geometric sans

### Typography Guidelines

- Letter-spacing: `0.05–0.15em` on headings for airy luxury
- Body text: `16–18px` with `1.6–1.8` line-height
- Optional accent script: *Great Vibes* or *Tangerine* — sparingly for taglines only
- Thin/light font weights feel delicate and refined

## 3. Visual Design Elements

### Textures & Patterns
- **Linen texture overlay** at `opacity: 0.03–0.06` on warm white backgrounds
- **Botanical line art** — single-line drawings of leaves, wildflowers, branches as decorative elements
- **Gold foil botanicals** — line art in `#C5A258` on cream backgrounds for premium sections
- **Thin gold divider lines** — 1px with small leaf ornament at center between sections
- **Soft watercolor washes** — sage green or dusty rose at 10-20% opacity for section dividers

### Photography Direction
- **Product:** Natural surfaces (raw linen, light marble, sandstone). Soft natural window light. Warm color temperature. 60-70% negative space.
- **Lifestyle:** Golden-hour outdoor light. Minimal makeup, effortless elegance. "A slow morning in a sunlit cottage."
- **Process:** Hands at the jeweler's bench, tools on worn wood, raw materials in natural light.
- **Treatment:** Rounded corners (8-12px), subtle warm overlay, hover zoom (scale 1.03-1.05)

### Product Description Formula

1. **Poetic opening** — one evocative sentence
2. **What it is** — materials, dimensions, practical details
3. **How it feels worn** — sensory, emotional
4. **Care or pairing note** — optional added value

**Example:**
> *A flash of northern lights, captured in stone and cradled in hand-wrapped copper wire.*
>
> Genuine labradorite cabochon wrapped in antiqued copper wire. 18" copper chain with handmade clasp.
>
> Wear it close to your heart on quiet days. Layer it with longer chains for a gathered, earthy look.
>
> *Each stone is unique — your piece may vary slightly, and that's part of its charm.*

### About Page Voice

First person. Specific. Sensory. Under 500 words. Ends with a personal sign-off:

> *"With love and steady hands, Lili"*

## 6. UX & Feature Priorities

### Essential Features
- **Image zoom / lightbox** — jewelry demands detail inspection
- **Multiple product angles** — minimum 4-6 images per piece
- **Material filtering** — by type, metal, stone, price, collection
- **Mobile-first design** — 60-70% of traffic from social/phones
- **Instagram feed integration** — social proof on homepage
- **Custom order inquiry form** — piece type, metal, budget, timeline, image upload
- **Newsletter signup** — "Letters from the Studio"

### Social Proof
- Testimonial section on homepage (3-5 rotating quotes)
- "Words from Those Who Wear Them" on product pages
- "As Seen In" press logo bar if applicable
- Customer photo gallery / UGC section

### SEO Essentials
- Product schema markup on every piece
- Descriptive image alt text and filenames
- Long-tail keyword targeting ("handmade gold hoop earrings")
- Blog content for organic traffic (care guides, material education, process stories)

## 7. Tech Stack

### Recommended Stack

| Layer            | Choice                    | Why                                          |
|------------------|---------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| **Framework**    | Astro                     | Zero JS by default, built-in image optimization, View Transitions, near-perfect Lighthouse scores |
| **Styling**      | Vanilla CSS + Custom Props | Full control, no bloat, theme variables      |
| **Animations**   | CSS transitions + IntersectionObserver | Lightweight scroll reveals, hover effects |
| **Smooth Scroll**| Lenis (~5KB)              | Luxurious scroll feel                        |
| **Gallery**      | PhotoSwipe v5 (~15KB)     | Best-in-class lightbox with zoom & gestures  |
| **Carousel**     | Embla Carousel (~6KB)     | Minimal, touch-friendly                      |
| **Page Transitions** | Astro View Transitions | Native crossfade/morph between pages        |
| **Hosting**      | Netlify or Cloudflare Pages | Free tier, CDN included                    |

### Key Animation Patterns

- **Fade-in on scroll** — elements materialize gently as user scrolls
- **Staggered grid reveal** — product cards cascade in with slight delays
- **Subtle hover zoom** — `scale(1.03)` with soft shadow on product images
- **Shimmer/gleam effect** — moving light highlight on hero imagery
- **View Transitions** — smooth crossfade between pages
- **Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`** — always

### Performance Targets
- Lighthouse score: 95-100
- Load time: under 2 seconds
- Image formats: AVIF → WebP → JPEG fallback
- Lazy loading on all below-fold images
- Blur-up placeholder technique for premium feel

## Summary

Lili's Creations website will be a **light, earthy, elegant** digital home that feels like stepping into a sunlit artisan studio. Every element — from the warm gold palette to the hand-drawn botanical accents to the intimate first-person copy — serves one purpose: to honor the handmade nature of Lili's craft and invite visitors to become part of her story.

The site will be fast (Astro), beautiful (curated typography & photography), and deeply personal (warm brand voice). It won't shout. It will hum.

> *"Jewelry that doesn't shout — it hums."*
